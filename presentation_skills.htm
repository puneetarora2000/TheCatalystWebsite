Presentation Skills

In order to progress in your career you need to develop yourself as an exceptionally good presenter.  What does it take to present your content and deliver it with panache.  We will cover both the designing and the delivery of the message.

Influencing Skills

As a manager or a leader you need to have influence over your stakeholders.  How do we influence someone else? This module will cover the skill and the techniques that will equip you to become an influential executive.

Management Development Program

The objective of the MDP is to help improve management systems by providing relevant training to executives of all levels in the industry, government and non-government sectors to take on a leadership role in their position individually and collectively.

  1. Team Building : Team building events that help you make your team work the way you     want it to. Effective, in-depth and fun team building programmes.
  2. Negotiation Skills Training : Take the anxiety out of Difficult Negotiations and learn how     to play the 'game' of Negotiations to solve conflicts at various levels.
  3. Stress Management : Dealing Effectively with Stress and learning how to manage     Stress.
