Conflict Management

Conflicts are a reality of today’s world.  In this module we take you through the different strategies of handling conflicts.  You will develop a thorough understanding of the various choices available to you for any given conflict situation.

Change Management

The only thing that is constant in our lives is Change.  The sooner we learn to adapt ourselves to this concept the better are going to be our chances of success.  The change management curve/matrix is explained with practical to do examples.

Negotiation Skills

In today’s tough competitive environment, one needs to have the power of getting one’s ideas or thinking across.  This is easier said than done.  The one skill that makes or breaks a deal is the art of effective negotiations.  Understanding yours as well as the other party’s bottom line will prepare you to plan and effectively execute the steps involved in negotiations.
